Gyotoku Area Guide

Getting There

  • Train: Tozai Line from Otemachi/Nihonbashi Station to Gyotoku Station (~30-40 min)
  • Walking: Most restaurants are within 5-10 min walk from Gyotoku Station

Nearby Attractions

  • Historic Canals: Edo-period waterways with traditional merchant houses
  • Gyotoku Bird Observatory: Nature spot for birdwatching
  • Traditional Streets: Narrow alleyways, wooden architecture, great for photography
  • Gyotoku Culture Hall I&I: Cultural events and exhibitions

Key Insights

  • Gyotoku has a notable Thai community, resulting in several authentic Thai restaurants
  • Most Thai restaurants cluster around Gyotoku Station (Ekimae area)
  • Lunch sets are very affordable (550-1,100 JPY range)
  • Some restaurants have closed recently — always check current status before visiting
  • Cash-only is common; bring JPY
