Key Insights

Ramen is King

Komuro dominates with 1,422 reviews, triple the runner-up. A Tabelog Ramen EAST 100 winner for three consecutive years.

Meat Paradise

4 of the top 10 are steak or meat-focused. Gyotoku loves its beef, from premium steak to casual meat bars.

BBQ Culture

3 yakiniku spots in the top 11 confirm a deep neighborhood love for grilled-at-table dining.

Walkable Dining

16 of 20 restaurants sit within 350 meters of Gyotoku Station. No car needed for a food crawl.

Cash Culture

The top two restaurants are cash-only. Old-school authenticity still rules at the top of the rankings.

Two Award Winners

Komuro (Ramen EAST 100) and Steak Ishii (Steak EAST 100) both hold Tabelog best-100 awards.
