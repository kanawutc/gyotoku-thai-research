Key Insights

  • Ramen is King: China Soba Komuro dominates with 1,422 reviews — 3x more than the #2 spot. A Tabelog Ramen 100 winner 3 years running.
  • Steak & Meat: 4 out of the top 10 are steak/meat restaurants. Gyotoku is a meat-lover's paradise.
  • Yakiniku Cluster: 3 yakiniku joints in the top 11 — BBQ culture is strong in this area.
  • Two 100 Winners: Both Komuro (Ramen) and Steak Ishii (Steak) are Tabelog 100 selections — rare for a single station.
  • Walkable: 16 out of 20 restaurants are within 350m of Gyotoku Station.
  • Cash Culture: Top 2 restaurants are cash-only. Bring JPY when visiting Gyotoku.
